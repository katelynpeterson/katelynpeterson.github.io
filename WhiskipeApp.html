The Helpful Kitchen Solution

Finding what to make with the ingredients avilable in my kitchen is a challenge and it takes up valuable time that I could be using to make the meal or do other projects.

The solution I came up with was creating an app that allows me to keep an inventory of the food I have in my house and get suggestions on recipes that use those on-hand ingredients. The idea is that it will free up time trying to figure out what I have and what I can make with it while avoiding making a trip to the store just to finish making the meal.
