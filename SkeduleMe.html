The Room Scheduling Solution

Purpose: Apply UX principles to design a room scheduler for the school to improve the current system and simplify the overall process.

We did interviews with prospective clients to gather requirements. With my team, we took the notes we gathered to create our list of requirements to build our system. We came up with a design that fulfilled the user requirements then created a prototype that we tested among our peers. We took the results from our test to improve our system and then presented our completed design to the class. Below are a diagram outlining the requirements and screen shots of our prototype.
